Interviews

Reflection

The interviews we conducted could have gone better. We were quite bias towards Blackboard from the start, and made this bias apparent during the interview. In hindsight this is shown by the way that our questions were formulated (like: "Do you like using Blackboard or can you think of improvements..?"). For recording purposes we should make sure to talk loud enough and instruct the interviewee to do the same.
Overall our questions were quite basic and just scratched the surface, we could ask better followup questions on good initial answers.

Improvements:

  • Biased questions
  • Not very effective questions
  • Ask better followup questions

About

The contract for Blackboard comes to an end, so the UvA (University of Amsterdam) will need a new student-education system in the short future. Software Engineering master students Tom van Duist, and Kevin van den Bekerom will extract relevant requirements for the new system by means of several assignments, which can be read on this blog.
